About the Ricestaurant

Ricestaurant was founded in 2018 by three rice innovators. The concept of the restaurant is simple! Rice is the most underrated carb but exists in more cuisines across the world than any other!* Our mission is to bring people together over a bowl of the good stuff. From risotto to paella, biryanis to rice crackers and everything inbetween - you saw it here first.
